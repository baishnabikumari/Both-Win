How to Play

  • You have to control two character at one time - 1st one is real world and 2nd one is mirror world.
  • Left right movement is oposite and up/down is same.
  • Both the character have to reach the glowing circles to complete the level.
  • Some walls are only in one world - use that to control movement.
  • There are 2 small switches which open the door in the other world.
  • Move slowly and carefully and think about how both the world react with one another.
